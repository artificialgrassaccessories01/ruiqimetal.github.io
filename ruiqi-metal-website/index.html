About Our Company

RQ Metal was established in 2005. We have two production bases in north of China, one in Tianjin City and another in Dingzhou City.

Our main factory covers an area of 90,000 square meters. Nail production capacity 15,000 tons annually, including common nails, brad head nails, roofing nails, U shape nails, etc; Galvanized wire production capacity 80,000 tons annually. We also have many products which using our iron wire as raw material, such as gopher wire, chain-link fence, and different kinds of welded wire mesh rolls and panels. We also provide customized service to meet the different needs of the customers.

We insist on that quality and cost are equally important in current market situation. We will try our best to provide the best quality products with the best prices. Hope we can be not only a business partner but also a lifetime friend.
